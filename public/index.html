Share life's moments and adventures

Globl Me is a mobile application that lets you browse, interact and share rich media with people around the world.

Adventure with friends,

Invite friends to join your adventures and upload photos together. Add your favorite photos as you go and view them all in one place, without the hassle of tracking down those important moments.

Follow what's important,

Enhance and customize your feed by following your friends, social groups and #tags that interest you. This keeps your feed tailored to your interests and leaves you with more time to explore.

Explore with ease.

Traverse through layers of the Globl stream, branching out from tag to tag. Jump from @user to #trip to #NYC to photos of Times Square with the touch of a finger. A truly non-stop browsing experience.
